Classes

At our gym, you can expect the best from our classes. Our teachers combined have over 100 years of experience in fitness instruction!

  • Mondays from 2-5pm: Future Yoga. Yoga has a long history, but we won’t confine ourselves to the past!
  • Tuesdays and Thursdays, 6-7pm: Lift This! An introduction to our gym and the equipment, tools, and attitudes that will make your fitness goals into reality!
  • Fridays from 6-7am: TGIFF: Thank Goodness It’s Fitness Friday! Learn to love Fridays with our super-early fitness hour of power.
  • Weekends from 6-9pm: Classical Cardio. Get your blood pumping to the subtle rhythms of Beethoven, Mozart, and Bach! Sweat to the real oldies!
